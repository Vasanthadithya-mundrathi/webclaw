🧠 Features

Everything OpenClaw does.
In your browser.

WebClaw brings the power of a full agentic runtime to any device with a browser — adding things OpenClaw never could.

🎮

Full Browser Tab Control

The WebClaw browser extension gives your agent access to all your tabs — read page content, click elements, fill forms, take screenshots, and automate any website. Just like OpenClaw's CDP, but no external tooling needed.

Extension Feature
⚡

WebGPU-Accelerated Compute

Vector indexing, semantic search, and embeddings run at GPU speed — entirely in your browser. Up to 30x faster than JavaScript alone. Your personal knowledge base searches in milliseconds.

World First
🧬

Gene Evolution Protocol

WebClaw learns from every task. Successful strategies are solidified into reusable "genes" that make your agent smarter over time. Ported from PicoClaw's CGEP with 6 seed genes and confidence scoring.

Self-Evolving
🔒

Trust Shield

Toggle security on or off. When ON, web content is tagged [UNTRUSTED] and can't modify your workspace or send messages without your approval. Per-skill permission levels: sandbox, confirm, or trusted.

Toggle ON/OFF
💾

OpenClaw-Compatible Workspace

Your workspace lives in your browser's OPFS filesystem — IDENTITY.md, USER.md, MEMORY.md, AGENTS.md. Same structure as OpenClaw and PicoClaw. Import your existing workspace in one click.

Migration Ready
📡

Channels via Thin Backend

Telegram, Discord, and Slack work through a 10-line backend you can deploy to Fly.io, Railway, or Render in one click. Or skip it — WebClaw works standalone in the browser.

Skills Ecosystem

🔒 Security

Security you control.
Not security that controls you.

OpenClaw's biggest known weakness is unstructured trust. WebClaw ships with Trust Shield — a simple toggle that gives you full control.

🛡️ Trust Shield
Controls whether external content can modify your workspace or trigger actions
SANDBOXED
Web content can't write workspace or trigger channels. Read-only from external sources.
CONFIRM
High-risk actions show an approval modal. You stay in the loop for anything important.
TRUSTED (OFF)
Full autonomous mode. Tools run without asking. Use only with trusted inputs.

A respectful request

WebClaw is not officially part of the Clawland ecosystem — yet. It's a student-led solo project, built by one person who learned AI agent architecture by reading through the OpenClaw and PicoClaw codebases.

The WebGPU compute approach, zero-install browser runtime, and Claw Mesh peer architecture are new ideas that don't exist in the current Clawland stack.
